About MoveSmart

MoveSmart is an innovative analytical tool designed to help individuals and companies make informed decisions about job mobility and financial planning. By integrating data on salaries, job offers, and cost of living across various cities and countries, MoveSmart empowers users to evaluate their career choices in a global context.

Key Features

Comprehensive Cost Analysis

Compare living costs, housing, transportation, food, and healthcare across different locations.

Salary Comparisons

Advanced salary comparisons by city and job sector.

Digital Nomad Insights

Identify optimal cities for digital nomads based on cost and quality of life.

Interactive Visualizations

Intuitive interface with interactive graphs for easy data interpretation.

Excel Report Export

Generate and export customized reports for further analysis.

How It Works

MoveSmart consolidates data from reliable sources to create a powerful analytical model. Users can:

  1. Input their job sector and current location
  2. Explore potential cities and compare living costs
  3. Evaluate salary expectations against local expenses
  4. Make informed decisions about relocation or remote work opportunities

Who Benefits

Professionals

Evaluate job offers and living standards across different cities

Companies

Set competitive salaries to attract global talent

Digital Nomads

Find the best cities that balance cost and quality of life

Recruiters

Understand salary trends and living costs for better talent acquisition

Market Analysts

Gain insights into economic trends affecting workforce mobility

Project Scope

MoveSmart focuses on providing static data analysis and does not include:

  • Real-time data updates
  • Long-term predictive models
  • Company-specific salary policies
  • Manual data collection from non-public sources

Our goal is to offer a representative sample of jobs, cities, and goods to support informed decision-making in the evolving landscape of global work.
